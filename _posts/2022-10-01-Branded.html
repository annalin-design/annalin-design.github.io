--- layout: post title: Branded Conference subtitle: Bringing youth entrepreneurs together in person. thumb: assets/images/thumb-branded.jpg description: Branded is a Youth Entrepreneurship Conference focused on Black, Newcomer and MVP (Most Vulnerable People) entrepreneurs between the ages of 18-30. After two years of lockdown, the Branded conference is back in person for 2022. Using the existing branding and logo, I was asked to design the conference identity and event collateral. The end result was a series of gradients leveraging Branded's colours, showcasing the magical blend of talents gathered at the conference. ---
